Questions

How does the $1.99 work?

You pay once when you click "Start a resume." That unlocks the editor for that resume — as many edits and PDF exports as you want. Starting a different resume is a separate $1.99.

Is my resume data stored anywhere?

No — everything happens in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to a server.

Is CV4ME free?

The editor is free to try with sample data via the live preview. Exporting your own finished resume as a PDF costs $1.99, paid once, with no subscription.

Will I have to pay again after I've already built my resume?

No. Unlike builders that let you complete a resume and then paywall the download, CV4ME asks for payment upfront — no surprise paywall at the end.

How is CV4ME different from Zety, Novoresume, or Canva?

Those are subscription services, typically $20 or more per month. CV4ME has no subscription and no account — $1.99 once per resume, nothing recurs.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, within 14 days if it's not a fit — just reply to your receipt email.
